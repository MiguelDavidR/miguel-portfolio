Horrible Imaginings Podcast

The Official Podcast of the Horrible Imaginings Film Festival

The Horrible Imaginings Podcast explores the emotional truth, artistic depth, and cultural relevance of horror and dark art. Through intimate, long-form conversations with filmmakers, authors, artists, and scholars, host Miguel Rodriguez creates a space for nuanced discussion around fear, beauty, storytelling, and the human experience. From independent cinema to genre-defying installations, the show delves beyond surface scares to uncover the passions and philosophies behind creative darkness.

Terry Castle, daughter of legendary filmmaker William Castle, shares anecdotes about her father's ingenious horror film gimmicks, his passion for storytelling, and the personal and professional challenges he faced.

Brian Hu joined me to do a deep dive on the programming choices at the 2017 San Diego Asian Film Festival, the importance of genre cinema, and so much more!
